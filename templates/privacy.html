Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, complete KYC verification, make a deposit, or communicate with support. This may include:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number).
  • Identity verification documents (Passport, ID card, utility bills).
  • Financial information (Wallet addresses, transaction history).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Process transactions and send related notifications.
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, two-factor authentication (2FA) support, and secure server infrastructure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with third-party service providers (e.g., email delivery services) only to the extent necessary to provide our services, or when required by law.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our site. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@vesthub.org.
